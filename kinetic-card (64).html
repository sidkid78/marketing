The Unprecedented Market Opportunity

HOMEase AI: Empowering Independent Living and Family Peace of Mind Through Innovative Aging-in-Place Solutions. As the global population ages, a significant demographic shift is reshaping societal needs. By 2030, the U.S. population over 65 is projected to reach 71 million, with an overwhelming majority desiring to 'age in place' within the comfort and familiarity of their own homes.

However, a stark 'Accessibility Gap' exists, as only a fraction of existing housing is structurally equipped for senior living. This critical intersection of desire and deficiency fuels a rapidly expanding market for aging-in-place (AIP) home modifications, projected to exceed $161 billion by 2030.

Data Insight

While 75% to 87% of older adults wish to remain in their homes, a mere 3.5% of U.S. residences are considered 'aging-ready'. This disparity drives a Total Addressable Market (TAM) for AIP solutions estimated at $105.8 billion in 2024.
