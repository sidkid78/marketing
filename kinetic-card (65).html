In an era where independent living and aging in place are increasingly prioritized, ensuring home safety is paramount. Healthcare professionals, including Occupational and Physical Therapists and Home Health Agencies, are critical guides in this landscape. They navigate patients and families through complex decisions about home safety and modification. However, their vital work often involves time-consuming assessments and manual documentation.

HOMEase offers a transformative solution. It is designed not to replace these experts, but to empower them with advanced tools that streamline processes, enhance recommendations, and ultimately, guide patients to safer living environments.

Empowering Experts
