The "Beautiful" Burden

Planning a baby shower is an act of love, but it often feels like a second job. Between the "Pinterest-pressure" to have a perfect theme and the logistical nightmare of tracking RSVPs, the joy of the occasion is often buried under a mountain of spreadsheets and decision fatigue. Most tools are just empty containers—they give you a list, but they don't give you ideas.

Passy

We believe that the woman carrying a life shouldn’t have to carry the stress of a spreadsheet. Passy is not just an app; it is a Creative Partner. We’ve harnessed the world’s most advanced AI not to replace the human touch, but to amplify it. We are here to eliminate "blank page syndrome" and logistical anxiety, turning the planning process into a celebration in itself.

Logic Serving Love

Your Personal Creative Director

Stop scrolling Pinterest for hours. Our AI-driven Theme Assistant doesn't just suggest names; it paints the vision. It listens to your story and generates bespoke, high-fidelity visualizations of your dream event in seconds.

End the Cringe

We’re retiring the awkward baby shower games of the past. Our Game Planner analyzes your specific guest list to curate activities that actually build connection, formatted and ready to print.

The "Gratitude Gap" Closed

The shower ends, but the work doesn’t—until now. Our Thank You Note Helper ensures that every guest feels truly seen, drafting personalized, elegant notes that reflect the specific joy of the gift received.
