Staring into an Abyss.

In 2024, I watched my grandmother stand at the edge of her own bathtub like she was staring into an abyss. I realized then that in the home-mod industry, a 'bad negotiation' isn’t a line item—it’s a shattered hip.

Wolves Circling.

It’s the moment a senior loses their independence forever because a contractor prioritized his margin over her safety. I founded HOMEase because I saw the wolves circling, and I refused to let my family, or yours, be the prey.

THE BOARDROOM

For years, I sat in the boardrooms of the Home Builders Association. I saw how the 'sausage was made.' I watched leaders obsess over profitability while ignoring the terrifying reality of a 70-year-old navigating a construction zone alone.

THE REALITY

I realized my 'technical background' wasn't for building companies; it was for building a shield. I don’t just vet contractors—I hunt for the few remaining artisans who still believe a stranger’s home is sacred ground.

Reclaiming
Dignity.

I’m not selling software or 'project management.' I am reclaiming dignity. I am filtering out the predators in contractor vests and elevating the craftsmen who treat your mother’s bathroom like a sanctuary.

A Fortress.

Because everyone deserves to age in the one place they still feel powerful: a home that isn't a booby trap, but a fortress.
