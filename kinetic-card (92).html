Eighth Circle of Hell:
Fraud

This circle is distinguished from its predecessors by being made up of those who consciously and willingly commit fraud.

Within the eighth circle is another called the Malebolge (“Evil Pockets”), which houses ten separate bolgias (ditches).

These ditches housed different types of people who committed fraud:

Panderers Seducers Flatterers Simoniacs Sorcerers Barrators Schismatics Alchemists Among Others

The Guardians

Each bolgia is guarded by different demons.

...and the inhabitants suffer different punishments, such as the simoniacs,

who stand head-first in stone bowls and endure flames upon their feet.
